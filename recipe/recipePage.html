Scalloped Potatoes Recipe

Scalloped Potatoes are an easy recipe, perfect for holidays and family dinners.


Photo Credit: SpendWithPennies.com

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 large onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 pounds white potatoes sliced about â…›" thick
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese

How to Make Scalloped Potatoes

  1. Thinly slice potatoes & onions.
  2. Make homemade sauce (recipe below)
  3. Layer potatoes, onions, cheese and sauce. Cover and bake.
  4. Remove foil and bake a bit more, this step creates the delicious brown top on the scalloped potatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.

Sauce

  1. To make the sauce, melt butter, onion and garlic over medium low heat.Cook until onion is softened, about 3 minutes.Add flower and cooke for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Reduce heat to low. Combine milk and broth. Add a small amount at a time whisking to thicken. The mixture will become very thick, continue adding a little bit of liquid at a time whisking unitl smooth.
  3. Once all of the liquid has been added, bring to boild over medium heat while continuing to whisk and adding the cup of cheddar cheese. Stir in salt and pepper and let boil for 1 minute.

Bring it all together

  1. Grease a 9" x 13 " baking dish. Place 1/3 of the potatoes in the bottom and season with salt and pepper. Pour 1/3 of the cream sauce over the top.
  2. Repeat layers ending with cream sauce. Cover and bake for 45 minutes.
  3. Allow to rest for 15 minutes before serving.
